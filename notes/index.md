## EN 🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿

This section collects structured technical notes on topics I am actively studying in cybersecurity and wireless systems in order to understand mechanisms deeply, analyze vulnerabilities rigorously, and connect theory with real-world security scenarios.

Each topic is approached with a consistent structure:

1. **Prerequisites** – required background knowledge and why it matters;
2. **Technical Mechanism** – how the system or protocol actually works (with diagrams when needed);
3. **Vulnerability Analysis** – where assumptions fail and security properties break;
4. **Real-World Scenario** – how the issue appears in practice;
5. **Defensive Considerations** – how the problem can be mitigated or prevented.
